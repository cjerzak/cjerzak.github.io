Technical & design choices

Intro loader

A sound gate that respects browser autoplay policies and stores your preference locally.

Smooth motion, reduced-motion support

Lenis smooth scroll is optional; reduced-motion disables transitions and continuous animation.

WebGL-inspired canvas

A lightweight “garden” field drawn on canvas (no heavy assets) with pointer-reactive sway.

SEO basics

Canonicals, Open Graph, sitemap, robots.txt, and consistent internal linking across routes.
